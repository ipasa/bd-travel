BD Travel

BD Travel Helper is helpful for a tourist and for local people in some purpose. It provides a list, description, location of tourist sports in Bangladesh division wise.

This app also help you by locating your location and location of the sport. Emergency services like Police, Hospital around Bangladesh are given in this app with location and contact.

This informations are helpful for local peoples too. A list of huge number of hotels are available in the app with location and contact.
